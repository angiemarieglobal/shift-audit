The Shift Audit

A 10-minute mirror that shows you where you are still negotiating with the version of you that you have already outgrown.

Before you begin

There is a decision you have already made. You made it quietly, a while ago. You know the version of you that you are becoming. You have already met that version. And yet, most days, you are still negotiating with the one you have outgrown.

What deal are you still striking with the version of you that says not yet.

A quiet deal gets struck. Not yet. Not fully. Not me. You move toward the thing, then talk yourself back down to a size that feels safe. It is not patience. It is a negotiation, and you have been losing it. This is not a test. It is a mirror.

The 90 second end of the negotiation

Put one hand on your chest. Both feet on the floor. One slow breath. Name the deal you keep striking with the version of you that says not yet. Then say the decision out loud, in your own voice.

I am no longer
negotiating with who I was.

Notice the part of you that immediately bargains back. The yes but, the not yet, the one more thing. That bargaining is the negotiation, live. You do not win it by negotiating harder. You end it by deciding. Say it again, an inch deeper.

The doorway

Most people will read this, feel the recognition, and strike the same deal again by morning. You do not have to be most people.

Shift is fourteen days where you stop negotiating with the old identity. Not more recognition. You decide, and you are held in the decision for fourteen days, until it is no longer a decision you are making but a person you have become.
